Job Profile: Marketing Manager

Job Title: Marketing Manager
Location: New York, NY
Department: Marketing
Reports To: Director of Marketing

Job Purpose:
The Marketing Manager is responsible for developing, implementing, and executing marketing strategies to increase brand awareness and market share. This role involves overseeing a small team, managing campaigns, and working closely with cross-functional departments to meet business objectives.

Key Responsibilities:

    Develop and execute comprehensive marketing strategies and plans.

    Manage marketing campaigns across various channels, including digital, print, and social media.

    Oversee the creation of marketing materials and content.

    Collaborate with product, sales, and customer service teams to ensure alignment.

    Analyze market trends and consumer insights to adjust strategies.

    Track performance metrics and report on campaign effectiveness.

Required Skills and Qualifications:

    Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, or related field.

    5+ years of experience in marketing or a related role.

    Strong understanding of digital marketing strategies and tools.

    Excellent project management and leadership skills.

    Proficient in marketing software (Google Analytics, Adobe Creative Suite, etc.).

    Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Desirable Skills:

    Master’s degree in Marketing or Business Administration.

    Experience in SEO and SEM.

    Knowledge of data analytics and consumer behavior.
